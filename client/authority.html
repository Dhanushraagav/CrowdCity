Operations Guide

  1. Review Cases: Select assigned complaint reports from your feed queue on the left.
  2. Inspect Location: Browse coordinates or address notes and deploy to inspect.
  3. Update Dispatch: Click the Update Status button to transition status to "In Progress" to inform citizens.
  4. Resolve & Prove: Once repairs are completed, set the status to Resolved, fill in resolution remarks, and upload a completion photo proof.
All updates dynamically notify citizens via their complaint tracking feeds.
