About CrowdCity

CrowdCity AI is a modern citizen-driven civic support platform. It empowers local communities to report public issues such as potholes, garbage, broken streetlights, and water leakage.

Using advanced AI, the platform auto-categorizes and routes reports to municipal authorities immediately, expediting resolution and keeping citizens updated in real-time.

Empower your city, report issues today.
