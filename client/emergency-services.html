Emergency Preparedness Guides

Official disaster preparedness protocols and immediate action steps.

Flood & Severe Waterlogging

Immediate Actions

  • Move to upper floors or higher ground immediately.
  • Switch off main electrical power and gas supply.
  • Keep emergency helpline 1070/1077 saved on your phone.

Things to Avoid

  • Do not walk or drive through moving flood water.
  • Do not touch electrical poles or fallen power lines.
  • Avoid consuming unboiled or contaminated tap water.
Fire & Building Evacuation

Immediate Actions

  • Call 101 immediately and raise fire alarm.
  • Crawl low under smoke to reach the nearest safe exit.
  • Close doors behind you to confine the fire.

Things to Avoid

  • Never use elevators during a fire evacuation.
  • Do not attempt to re-enter a burning structure.
  • Do not use water on electrical or grease fires.
Earthquake Protocols

Immediate Actions

  • Drop, Cover, and Hold On under sturdy furniture.
  • If outdoors, stay in an open area away from buildings.
  • Protect your head and neck with your arms.

Things to Avoid

  • Do not rush for staircases or exit doors while shaking.
  • Avoid standing near glass windows or heavy shelves.
Cyclone & Storm Preparedness

Immediate Actions

  • Secure loose roof sheets and window shutters.
  • Keep battery-powered radio and emergency lights ready.

Things to Avoid

  • Do not venture outside during the eye of the storm.

Medical First-Aid Reference

Basic emergency reference instructions while awaiting medical response.

CPR Basics

  • Check for responsiveness and breathing.
  • Push hard and fast in center of chest (100–120 bpm).
  • Continue until professional medical help arrives.

Severe Bleeding

  • Apply firm, continuous direct pressure with clean cloth.
  • Elevate the wounded limb above heart level.
  • Do not remove soaked bandages; add more layers.

Burns

  • Cool burn with cool running water for 10–20 minutes.
  • Cover loosely with a clean, sterile bandage.
  • Do not apply ice, butter, or ointments.

Fractures

  • Immobilize the injured limb immediately.
  • Do not attempt to realign broken bones.
  • Apply ice pack wrapped in cloth to reduce swelling.
